About Us

Welcome to Thrifty! We are dedicated to bringing you the best thrift shopping experience. Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable clothing and accessories while promoting sustainable fashion.

Our Story

Thrifty was founded in 2024 with the aim of creating a platform for thrift shops in Kenya. Thrifty aims to revolutionize the thrift shopping landscape in Kenya by creating an accessible, user-friendly, and comprehensive online marketplace. Our vision is to connect consumers with a diverse range of thrift shops, enabling them to discover, compare, and purchase unique items from the comfort of their homes..
